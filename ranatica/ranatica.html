What is the Ranatica?

The Ranatica is a project with 2 complementary objectives:
  • Make people familiar with roman latin
  • Make people familiar with roman comedy
It can be found at https://github.com/s0stratos/ranatica.

What can be found in the Ranatica

The Ranatica is an ever-evolving project but over time 5 major lines of work have emerged.

RANATICA

This section is about using roman latin grammar to express oneself

LUDI

This section is about game resources to learn and rehearse roman latin.

FABULAE

This section is about annotated texts. for various purposes. as well as other large scale "raw" data.

SCITIN FORO UTI

This section is about knowing how to use words and idioms in roman (comedy) latin.

UAE ME QUI RECENS IN FORUM SUM DELATUS

This section is about being able to talk about specific topics in roman (comedy) latin.
